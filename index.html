Our Paint Collection

From premium finishes to specialty textures, we have the perfect paint for every surface and style.

Emulsion

Our signature water-based paint, providing a smooth, washable matte finish. Perfect for interior walls and ceilings.

Gloss

A high-sheen, durable finish that's resistant to moisture and stains. Ideal for kitchens, bathrooms, and woodwork.

Texcote

A high-build textured finish designed for exterior walls. Provides excellent weather protection and covers imperfections.

Satin / Silk

An elegant, mid-sheen finish that offers a soft glow. It's durable and easy to clean, great for high-traffic areas.

Matt

A non-reflective, modern finish that hides surface imperfections. Delivers deep, rich color for a sophisticated look.

P.O.P. Paint

Specially formulated for Plaster of Paris ceilings and designs. Provides a brilliant white, smooth, and even finish.
